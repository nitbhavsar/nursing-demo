About Us

Merchant Institute of Nursing – Piludara, managed by Merchant Education Trust, Visnagar, stands as a symbol of academic excellence, ethical values, and commitment to healthcare education.

With a strong foundation built on trust, integrity, and service, we strive to prepare students who are not only academically competent but also socially responsible healthcare providers ready to serve hospitals and communities alike.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in nursing education by developing highly skilled, ethical, and compassionate nursing professionals who serve society with dedication and care — enabling students to undertake responsibilities and cope with challenges through active learning and holistic health practice.

Our Mission

  • Provide qualitative education in both theoretical knowledge and practical skills
  • Create a learning environment that promotes critical thinking and professional growth
  • Offer comprehensive hospital and community-based clinical exposure
  • Develop nurses who are confident, competent, and committed to patient care
  • Produce efficient and highly capable nurses who meet modern healthcare standards
  • Instil in students a spirit of dedication and commitment

Programs Offered

Four recognised nursing programs designed to shape career-ready healthcare professionals.

B.Sc. Nursing

Intake: 60 Seats

Bachelor's program building a strong clinical and academic nursing foundation.

G.N.M.

Intake: 60 Seats

General Nursing & Midwifery – comprehensive hospital-based training.

A.N.M.

Intake: 40 Seats

Auxiliary Nursing & Midwifery – focused on primary and community healthcare.

Post Basic B.Sc.

Intake: 50 Seats

Advanced degree for practising GNM nurses seeking higher qualification.

Spread across 30+ acres, Merchant Education Campus is an education hub featuring well-designed buildings, contemporary laboratories, a modern fully-equipped auditorium, and rich recreational facilities that make it one of the finest in the region.

Students come from diverse backgrounds across the city and region. The institute is committed to making education accessible to all through scholarships, financial aid, and a full spectrum of extra-curricular, co-curricular, and athletic opportunities.
