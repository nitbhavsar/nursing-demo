How to Apply

Admission Process

A simple four-step process to secure your seat at Merchant Institute of Nursing.

01

Check Eligibility

Review the eligibility criteria for your desired program and confirm you meet the academic and age requirements.

02

Contact Admissions

Call our admissions helpline or visit the campus to collect the application form and get guidance on document requirements.

03

Submit Documents

Submit your completed application form along with the required documents. Our team will verify and process your application.

04

Confirm Your Seat

Upon selection, complete the fee payment and document submission to confirm your admission and secure your seat.

Be Prepared

Documents Required

Please keep the following documents ready when applying. Originals will be required for verification at the time of admission.

  • 10th Mark Sheet & Passing Certificate
  • 12th Mark Sheet & Passing Certificate
  • Aadhaar Card (original + photocopy)
  • Recent Passport Size Photographs (6 copies)
  • Medical Fitness Certificate
  • Transfer Certificate from previous institution
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • GNM Registration Certificate (Post Basic only)
