About the Program

The Auxiliary Nursing & Midwifery (A.N.M.) diploma is a two-year program that trains students as frontline healthcare workers in community health, primary care and maternal and child health services.

ANM graduates play a critical role in rural and semi-urban healthcare delivery, working as health workers, community nurses and midwives within the public health system.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed 10+2 or equivalent from a recognised board
  • Minimum age of 17 years as on 31st December of admission year
  • Must have passed English as a subject
  • Medically fit as per Gujarat Nursing Council norms
  • Female candidates only (as per INC / GNC norms)

Program Structure

  • Community Health Nursing
