About the Program

The Bachelor of Science in Nursing (B.Sc. Nursing) is a four-year undergraduate degree program that equips students with in-depth theoretical knowledge and extensive practical skills in nursing sciences, healthcare principles, and patient care.

At Merchant Institute of Nursing, the program is designed to develop highly skilled, ethical, and compassionate nursing professionals who are ready to serve in hospitals, community health settings, and specialised healthcare environments.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry and Biology
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks (40% for reserved categories)
  • Minimum age of 17 years as on 31st December of admission year
  • Must have passed English as a subject at 10+2 level
  • Medically fit as per INC norms
