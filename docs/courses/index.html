Academic Programs

Our Nursing Programs

Four recognised nursing programs designed to shape skilled, ethical and career-ready healthcare professionals.

Undergraduate 4 Years 60 Seats

Bachelor of Science in Nursing (B.Sc. Nursing)

A four-year undergraduate degree program that prepares students with comprehensive theoretical knowledge and extensive clinical skills in nursing sciences, healthcare principles and patient care.

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry & Biology (min. 45%)
  • Age: Minimum 17 years as on 31st December of admission year
  • Affiliated to Hemchandracharya North Gujarat University (HNGU)
  • Approved by Indian Nursing Council & Gujarat Nursing Council
View Full Details Apply Now
Diploma 3 Years 60 Seats

General Nursing & Midwifery (G.N.M.)

A three-year diploma program providing thorough training in general nursing and midwifery, combining hospital-based clinical practice with strong theoretical foundations in healthcare.

  • Eligibility: 10+2 from a recognised board (min. 40%)
  • Age: Minimum 17 years as on 31st December of admission year
  • Recognised by Gujarat Nursing Council
  • Extensive hospital-based clinical training included
View Full Details Apply Now
Diploma 2 Years 40 Seats

Auxiliary Nursing & Midwifery (A.N.M.)

A two-year diploma program focused on primary and community healthcare, midwifery and basic nursing skills. Ideal for those seeking to serve at the grassroots level of healthcare delivery.

  • Eligibility: 10+2 from a recognised board
  • Age: Minimum 17 years as on 31st December of admission year
  • Focus on community health and preventive care
  • Recognised by Gujarat Nursing Council
View Full Details Apply Now
Post Basic 2 Years 50 Seats

Post Basic Bachelor of Science in Nursing (Post Basic B.Sc.)

A two-year advanced degree program exclusively for practising GNM-qualified nurses seeking to upgrade their qualifications to a bachelor's degree and enhance their clinical and leadership capabilities.

  • Eligibility: GNM qualified with minimum 1 year of work experience
  • Registered with State Nursing Council
  • Affiliated to Hemchandracharya North Gujarat University (HNGU)
  • Approved by Indian Nursing Council
