About the Program

The Post Basic Bachelor of Science in Nursing is a two-year degree program designed exclusively for registered nurses who hold a GNM diploma and wish to upgrade to a bachelor's degree.

This program deepens clinical knowledge, enhances leadership skills, and opens doors to senior nursing roles, teaching positions and specialised career paths in India and internationally.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must hold a GNM diploma from a recognised institution
  • Minimum one year of work experience as a registered nurse
  • Registered with State Nursing Council
  • Passed 10+2 or equivalent from a recognised board
  • Medically fit as per INC norms

Program Structure

Year 1
  • Advanced Nursing Practice
  • Nursing Research & Statistics
  • Nursing Informatics
  • Advanced Pharmacology
  • Clinical Specialisation
Year 2
