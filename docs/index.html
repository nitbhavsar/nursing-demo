Who We Are

About Merchant Institute of Nursing

Merchant Institute of Nursing – Piludara, managed by Merchant Education Trust, Visnagar, is affiliated to Hemchandracharya North Gujarat University (HNGU) and recognised by the Government of Gujarat and Gujarat Nursing Council.

We prepare skilled, ethical and compassionate nursing professionals through strong academic foundations and extensive clinical training in hospitals and community healthcare environments.

Learn More About Us

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in nursing education by developing highly skilled, ethical, and compassionate nursing professionals who serve society with dedication and care.

Our Mission

  • Provide quality theoretical and practical nursing education
  • Promote critical thinking and professional development
  • Offer hospital and community-based clinical exposure
  • Develop confident and competent nursing professionals
  • Produce efficient nurses who meet modern healthcare standards
