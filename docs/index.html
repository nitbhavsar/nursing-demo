About the College

Merchant Institute of Nursing, managed by Merchant Education Campus, is affiliated to Hemchandracharya North Gujarat University (HNGU) and recognized by the Government of Gujarat, Indian Nursing Council (New Delhi) and Gujarat Nursing Council.

We prepare skilled, ethical and compassionate nursing professionals through strong academic foundations and extensive clinical training in hospitals and community healthcare environments.
